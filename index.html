About Us

At PLBN Watches, we are passionate about bringing luxury watches and jewellery to our clients in the UAE. Established in 2020, we are a pre-owned watches and jewellery trader based in the UAE, and we pride ourselves on offering an exquisite selection of pieces from world-renowned brands.

We are not affiliated with any of the luxury brands we sell, but we do guarantee the authenticity and quality of all our products. Our team of experts meticulously inspects every item in our inventory to ensure that it meets our strict standards of excellence.

At PLBN Watches, we offer a seamless and personalized experience to all our clients, whether you're looking to buy, sell, or trade. We understand that luxury watches and jewellery are more than just accessories – they are investments in your personal style and taste. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure that every client receives the highest level of service and attention.

Thank you for considering PLBN Watches for your luxury watch and jewellery needs. We look forward to serving you and helping you discover the timeless elegance of our collection.
